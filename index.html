What they offer

Simple barber services. Done right.

Come in for the usual cleanup, a clean fade before the weekend, beard work, a classic men’s cut, or a kids cut. The shop has that darker old-school feel — music on, vintage stuff on the walls, barbers who keep it straightforward.

Haircuts

Regular men’s haircuts, scissor cuts, short styles, longer cleanups, and everyday maintenance cuts.

Fades & Tapers

Skin fades, low fades, mid fades, tapers, neckline cleanup, and sharp blends.

Beard Trims

Shape-ups, beard lineups, cleanup around the cheeks and neck, and balanced beard shaping.

Straight Razor Work

Razor edges, clean lines, neckline detail, and classic barbershop finishing work.

Kids Cuts

Good for kids and families who want a normal, reliable shop without making the appointment a whole production.

Classic Styles

Side parts, gentleman’s cuts, business cuts, slick styles, and product advice when you need it.
